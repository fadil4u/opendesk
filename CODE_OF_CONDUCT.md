# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in opendesk a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background, experience level, or identity.

## Our standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Welcome different viewpoints and experiences
- Give and accept feedback graciously
- Focus on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers at **abhijith@vitalops.ai**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
